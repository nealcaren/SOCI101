First Week Checklist¶

By the end first week of class, be sure to:

  • Purchase the textbook, Real World, either through the student store or online.

  • Optional) Complete How To Use InQuizitive.

  • Sign up for the course Slack space.

  • Let me know that you read the Code of Conduct before class on Thursday by completing the Sakai assignment.

  • Attend class on Thursday on the course Slack site.

  • Complete Chapter 1 InQuizitive assignment by Sunday night.

  • Submit The Derby discussion notes by Sunday night.
